## FAQ

### What is my IP?

Your public IP address is the address websites see when your browser connects to them.

### How do I check if my proxy is working?

Open Ping123 while connected to your proxy and compare the displayed IP, location, ASN, and risk signals with your expected proxy information.

### Can a VPN leak my real IP?

Yes. VPN leaks can happen through DNS, WebRTC, browser settings, or routing misconfiguration. Ping123 helps you check these manually.

### What is a DNS leak?

A DNS leak happens when DNS requests are sent outside your VPN or proxy tunnel, potentially exposing your real network provider.

### What is a WebRTC leak?

A WebRTC leak happens when browser WebRTC APIs expose local or network-related information unexpectedly.
